Introduction

Pokédex is a webpage designed to catalog and provide information regarding the various species of Pokémon. It has valuable information for Pokémon Trainers, as it helps them learn about the characteristics, habitats, and abilities of different Pokémon. So, lets start exploring the world of Pokémon through this Pokédex!

P.S.:Since it is a demo version, only the starter pokemons are listed here. We promise to add more pokemons in future updates. Please stay tuned! Thank you for your understanding!:)
